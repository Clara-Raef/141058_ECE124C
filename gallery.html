Zuhair Murad Couture - Spring Summer 2020 Collection

Nefertiti, Nefertari, Isis or Berenice: for his Couture Spring-Summer 2020 collection, Zuhair Murad pays homage to Egyptian queens who left a mark on history by their beauty and power. Each piece is elevated into an exploration of timeless elegance tinted with magic. It is a true game of opacity and transparency that is at play, that allows light to shine through in each design. A result embellished by Egyptian symbolism, that infuses a mystical touch to the collection: the beetle, the incarnation of the sun and fertility, the lotus flower which suggests renewal or the cat, as a mark of divine love.
